DR. B.R. AMBEDKAR: INDIAN JURIST, ECONOMIST, AND SOCIAL REFORMER


BIOGRAPHY

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar was an Indian jurist, economist, and social reformer who campaigned against social discrimination towards the untouchables (Dalits) and also supported the rights of women and workers. He was the principal architect of the Indian Constitution and served as India's first Law Minister.
